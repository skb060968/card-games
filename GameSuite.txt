2. Bluff

Objective

Discard all cards without being successfully challenged.

Gameplay Flow

1   Select cards 2   Declare rank
3   Place cards face-down 4   Others may challenge 5   Resolve challenge
6   Pass turn

Core Logic

1   Store actual cards separately 2   Validate only on challenge
3   Assign pile to loser
3. Flip & Match

Objective

Collect most cards via matching ranks.

Core Logic


1   Only one face-up card per rank 2   Match removes both cards
3   No memory mechanic 4. Perfect Ten

Objective

Collect ranks 1–10.

Core Logic

1   Draw and discard each turn 2   Track ranks collected
3   Check win condition after discard

2   Bet / Call / Raise / Fold
3   After one round enable Show 4   Show reveals cards

3   Compare hands
